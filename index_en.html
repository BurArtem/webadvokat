Who are we?

We are a law firm that solves the most pressing problems of today. No fluff, no complex legal jargon, no empty promises. Just a clear action plan and results-driven work. Behind us are over 20 years of rigorous practice and more than 1500 won cases.

Today, we focus all our efforts on protecting your interests where it matters most:

  • ✓ TCC and Mobilization: We ensure lawful protection. Support, appeals of VLC decisions, deferment issues, and full legal control of the situation. We handle all communications.
  • ✓ Driver Protection (Auto Lawyer): We get you out of complex accidents, cancel unlawful protocols, and restore driver's licenses (including under Article 130 of the Code of Administrative Offenses).

Additionally, we continue to successfully resolve all matters related to family, property, and commercial law — from complex divorces and property division to comprehensive protection of your business.
